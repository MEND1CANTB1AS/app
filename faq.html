FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How long will it take to get my PC to me?

A: It will take approximately 2 weeks for your PC to reach you after you have submitted your order. A week to assemble your PC and another week for shipping.

Q: How much is shipping?

A: Shipping is free!

Q: How can I check the status of my order?

A: Once your order is sent you will be emailed your tracking number. You will then be able to view the progress of your package.

Q: Do you ship outside of the US?

A: Currently we are only shipping to locations within the United States. We apologize for any inconvenience.

Q: Can I cancel my order?

A: Yes, orders can be cancelled before they are shipped to you.

Q: Can I return my order and get a refund?

A: Yes, orders can be returned for a full refund if returned within 2 weeks of the delivery date.

Q: If something is wrong with my order, who do I contact?
