Overview

Purpose

Have you ever tried to or wan to assemble build your own computer? Having a computer that can handle the tasks that you require of it has become an essential part of todays high speed world. Being able to control exactly what goes into your own personal computer can be very beneficial. However, not all PC parts are compatible with each other. Our goal at Performance PC is to take the guess work out of building a PC. We vet each and when you decide on a part to add to your computer, we will only show you other parts that are compatible. Once you have selected the parts you desire, you can have them all shipped to you for your building pleasure or you can have us assemble it for you professionally. Whether for business or fun you can count on our expertise to help pick the parts that are right for you.

Audience

We aim to serve the PC enthusiast. Whether you are a gamer or a professional video or photo editor, our goal is to build you the PC you need. Using our simple questionnaire, we ask you what your desired focus is and we will give you our expert recommendation. But feel free to modify your PC to your liking. Once you have finished selecting your parts you can have us assemble it or we can send the parts to you to assemble to your liking.

Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?

Our site will have an easy to use interface and we will help direct the customer to get the parts they need. Other websites assume that the user knows beforehand what they want but we intend to help the user fill in any gaps in their PC build that they might have. We will also insure that the parts are compatible. Finally, we will offer them the option to build the PC themselves or have us build it for them. If they decide to build it all parts will be shipped together and will arrive ready for assembly with a basic instruction manual/guide.
